  • Category: Competition Entry

  • Brief: To create an entry for the Rookies competition based on the world of D.R.E.W.

  • Link: Rookies Submission

  • I worked in a team to create an entry for the Rookies competition. Based on the story of two robots, this piece of work took more than a year to make as we worked on this in our spare time, in addition to our study and work commitments. I was responsible for the smoke, foam, and spark FX in the sequence. This entry won the Bronze Student Prize in the Australian Effects and Animation Festival in 2022.
