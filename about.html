About

Lok Sang is a 3D Artist whose work combines realism with sci-fi elements. He is a team player who can also work independently on projects. He has worked in teams where the work has been shown in Vivid Sydney, Sydney Film Festival and has won accolades from the Australian Effects and Animation Festival.

Lok Sang has masters degrees in telecommunications engineering and animation/visualisation from the University of Sydney and University of Technology, Sydney, as well as a graduate diploma and bachelor degrees in interactive multimedia, engineering (telecommunications), 3D animation and commerce (management). He has trained for a year as an FX artist using Houdini at the Animal Logic Academy, and is currently involved in an independent project working with Blender and Unity. Lok Sang is also highly skilled in computer programming.

He is currently working full-time as a Technical Assistant with Industrial Light and Magic. Lok Sang has appeared as a credit on the Disney Plus streaming series, Obi-Wan Kenobi.

His software skills include Blender, Houdini, Unity and Python.
